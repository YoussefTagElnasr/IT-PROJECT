About Dr, Youssef

Dr. Youssef Nagah is a trusted dental surgeon with 7 years of focused experience in surgical dentistry. He holds a Bachelor's Degree in Oral and Dental Medicine and has completed advanced training in oral surgery, including dental implant placement, surgical extractions, and minor maxillofacial procedures. Dr. Nagah is known for his meticulous technique, steady hand, and commitment to patient safety. He takes the time to ensure every patient fully understands their treatment plan and feels at ease before, during, and after surgery. Currently practicing at our clinic, Dr. Nagah brings both precision and compassion to every procedure. His dedication to ongoing education and the latest surgical advancements allows him to provide the highest standard of care in a calm, professional environment.

Surgical Tooth Extractions, Dental Implants, Minor Oral Surgery, Bone Grafting & Ridge Preservation, Pre-Prosthetic Surgery and Management of Oral Surgical Emergencies
