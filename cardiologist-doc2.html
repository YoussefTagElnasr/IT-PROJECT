About Dr, ElKhateb

Dr. Tarek El Khateb is a 45 year old respected cardiologist with 15 years of experience in adult cardiovascular care. Known for his balanced approach to patient care, Dr. Tarek combines up-to-date medical knowledge with a personable, respectful manner that puts patients at ease. He earned his cardiology specialization from a top-tier university hospital and has managed hundreds of complex heart conditions with confidence and clarity. Dr. El Khateb emphasizes early detection, preventive care, and personalized treatment strategies. He listens attentively and encourages open communication, making sure patients leave the clinic understanding their heart health. His patients often praise his professionalism, though his schedule can be busy due to high demand.
