About Dr, Fahmy

Dr. Maha Fahmy is a 35 year old certified dermatologist with 7 years of experience in treating skin, hair, and nail conditions. She has a strong foundation in clinical dermatology and offers a range of non-invasive cosmetic procedures. Dr. Maha is committed to helping patients feel more comfortable in their skin but has received mixed feedback regarding communication and clinic flow.While many patients trust her medical judgment and appreciate her straightforward style, others feel that their visits were sometimes rushed or lacked follow-up. She continues to focus on improving the patient experience while maintaining high standards in clinical care.
