About Dr, Moahmed

Dr. Leen Mohamed is a dedicated dermatologist with 9 years of experience in medical, cosmetic, and laser dermatology. She is known for her gentle approach, personalized treatment plans, and commitment to helping patients feel confident in their skin. Dr. Leen believes that healthy skin is not just about appearance, but about comfort, self-esteem, and overall wellness. Her clinical approach is thorough and patient-centered, offering both evidence-based medical treatments and minimally invasive aesthetic procedures. Whether you're dealing with acne, chronic skin conditions, or looking to enhance your skin's radiance, Dr. Leen combines skill with sincerity in every consultation.
